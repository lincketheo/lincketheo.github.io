Meet Theo Lincke - Your Partner in Software

About Theo Lincke

I'm a Software Engineer with a focus on clean software and system architecture, driven by a love for breaking problems down into small tangible units. My background spans digital signal processing, full-stack development, and embedded systems, and I have a deep commitment to building secure, reliable software. I really like the Unix philosophy (keeping things simple, and building software tools that work in tandem with one another).

Outside of work, I stay active-country swing dancing at Clarendon Ballroom in Arlington, hiking and camping off-grid, and spending time lifting or swimming. I also enjoy woodworking and diving into a good Star Wars novel.

Mission Statement

To help users view the world with openness and clarity through unbiased data visualization, empirical evidence, and the spirit of curiosity. By crafting simple, accessible tools, I want to empower my users to explore and understand their domain better. I stick to the basics, and believe you can do a lot with a little with software.

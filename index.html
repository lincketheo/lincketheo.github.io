About

As a software engineer, I prioritize building impactful, user-centered solutions based on the following core principles:

  1. Prioritize the User: Technology should adapt to user needs, not the other way around. Make decisions that enhance user experience, utility, and satisfaction within the company's time, capital, and energy constraints.
  2. Simplicity and Usability: Engineers often create unnecessary complexity. Automate or abstract only when it improves the product; complexity without purpose is counterproductive.
  3. Ethical Data Fuels AI: AI relies on quality, transparent data. Create tools that openly display data collection, avoiding hidden or implicit practices.
  4. The Value of Skilled Engineers: Frameworks like Agile provide structure, but skilled, principled engineers drive success. Elevate skills, minimize ego, and respect others' contributions.
  5. Stay Relevant, But Selective: Ruthlessly filter which solutions stay top of mind. Focus on the most valuable, current tools, and avoid outdated or redundant practices.
